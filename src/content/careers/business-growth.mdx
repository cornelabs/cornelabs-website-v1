🚀 **Join Us in Building the Future of Software Development**  

### **About Us**  
We are a fast-growing software agency on a mission to build world-class custom software solutions for businesses across the globe. While we start by delivering high-quality software for clients, our long-term vision includes launching a portfolio of SaaS products and AI-driven solutions. We are looking for highly motivated **Business Growth Consultants** to help us land new projects and expand our reach across North America, Europe, and the Middle East.  

### **The Role**  
We need **results-driven deal closers** who can identify and secure high-value projects for our agency. This is a **fractional, commission-based role**—you’ll be rewarded generously for every contract you bring in. The more deals you close, the more you earn. If you’re an ambitious business development expert looking for a **high-impact opportunity with long-term potential**, this is for you!  

### **Responsibilities**  
✅ Identify and secure **custom software development** projects for businesses in various industries.  
✅ Develop and execute **client acquisition strategies** for international markets (North America, Europe, and the Middle East).  
✅ Build relationships with **startups, enterprises, investors, vc firms, venture studios, and more** looking to develop MVPs and new software products.  
✅ Explore potential **partnerships and collaborations** that could drive revenue growth.  
✅ Work closely with the founder to refine our market positioning and revenue strategy.  
✅ Negotiate contracts and ensure smooth onboarding of new clients.  

### **What We’re Looking For**  
✔ Proven experience in **business development, sales, or revenue growth** within tech or software services.  
✔ Strong network and ability to connect with decision-makers in target markets.  
✔ Self-starter with a **hunter mentality**—you thrive on winning deals.  
✔ Experience selling **custom software solutions, SaaS, or AI-driven products** is a plus.  
✔ Familiarity with global markets, especially **Europe, the Middle East, or North America**.  
✔ Excellent negotiation and relationship-building skills.  
✔ Ability to work independently and adapt to a fast-changing environment.  

### **Compensation & Growth Opportunities**  
💰 **Commission-based earnings**—earn a **percentage of every contract closed** (higher incentives for bigger deals).  
📈 **Opportunity for full-time leadership roles** as we scale.  

### **Why Join Us?**  
✔ Work with a visionary founder set on building a **global tech powerhouse**.  
✔ Shape the **early growth** of a company with massive potential.  
✔ Freedom to **set your own strategy** and win deals your way.  
✔ Be part of a company that values **innovation, impact, and long-term success**.  

🔹 **How to Apply**  
If you’re excited about this opportunity and believe you can drive business growth, send us an email to hello@cornelabs.com with your **experience, past successes, and why you’re the perfect fit!**
